Renaissance Man

I consider myself to be something of a 'Renaissance Man', so to speak. My deep love of learning and curiosity for life has led me down many different paths where I have experienced so many wonderful things

I first starting dipping my toes into Marketing in college. As part of my financial aid, I was given a work study in the Marketing Department and loved it! I even got to do some design work. Even though I was completely into my business and marketing classes, I wanted to find something that would directly make the world a better place. I just wasn't sure what that was yet.

Just out of college, I landed a job that was a dream as a preschool and senior portrait photographer. I traveled around Virginia taking school photos and Senior photos. In addition to developing my creativity and skills as a photographer, I also got to start to put into real-life practice what I learned in my business and marketing classes in college. Sadly though, this dream was not one to last. The company didn't have it in the budget to keep me on.

From there, I came out to Colorado where I attended massage school at Boulder College of Massage Therapy. One of the things I enjoy most about being a Massage Therapist is knowing that physiologically I'm able to see, and literally feel, the changes in someone's body from a massage. Through opening my own business, Felt Massage, I got to learn even more business and marketing skills. Although, doing massage full-time is much more difficult on my body than I was expecting and have found it's something that works best as a part-time gig.
